## Supply and Demand: The Fundamentals

![Supply vs Demand Chart](/silver-supply-demand.png)

Here's where it gets interesting. The silver market has been running massive deficits for five straight years:

| Year | Supply (M oz) | Demand (M oz) | Deficit (M oz) |
|------|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| 2021 | 897          | 1,052         | -155           |
| 2022 | 822          | 1,187         | -365           |
| 2023 | 843          | 1,246         | -403           |
| 2024 | 848          | 1,268         | -820*          |

*The 2024 deficit includes carryover from previous years.

**What this means:** We're using silver faster than we can mine it, and the deficit is accelerating.
